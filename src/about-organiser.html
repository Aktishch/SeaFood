Who we are?
Seafood Expo Development is a company specialized in events for fishery industry and licensed to hold large-scale events in Turkey. We are international team with diverse organizing experience.
Same as our exhibitions we represent different cultures: we speak Turkish, English, German, Russian, French, Chinese, Ukrainian, Uzbek and Spanish. Different cultural background helps us better understand the needs of our clients. For us making events is not just a job but lifelong story.
