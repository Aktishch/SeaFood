GLOBAL FISHERY FORUM is the main congress event of the Russian fishery industry. Having an international importance, it focuses on key issues of development of the fishery industry and related sectors, economic efficiency and social stability, regulation of fishing and competition.This year the main topics will be inner market and industry infrastructure: shipbuilding, deep processing, logistics and storage and aqua- and mariculture.
Among forum topics 2021:
  • Catching and processing
  • Building and designing of fishery fleet
  • import and trading enterprises
  • Logistics, including organization of uninterrupted cold chain
  • Aquaculture
  • Teaching new generation of russian fish consumers
  • Learning new markets and development of international cooperation
  • Human and scientific resources of the industry
